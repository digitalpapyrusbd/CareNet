# Compliance & Metrics

## Legal & Compliance
- Data storage jurisdiction: within Bangladesh or AWS-approved regions.  
- Consent and privacy policy for patients and guardians.  
- Background verification linked to National ID / Police check.  
- Health data encrypted at rest and in transit.  
- Data retention: 5 years (default).
